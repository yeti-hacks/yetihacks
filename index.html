    A hackathon for social good

    October 15-17

    About YETI Hacks:

    YETI Hacks is a high school virtual hackathon organized by the Youth for Ethical Technology Institute, a student-led initiative that aims to advance technology for social good. Students will have the opportunity to create a product that harnesses technology’s power to improve society, while enjoying workshops, events, speaker panels, swag, and more at no cost!


    Prize Tracks

    Overall Top Three Hacks

    The overall top three hacks that positively impact society will be given special prizes and cool opportunities!

    Best Ethical Technology Hack

    This prize will be given to the hack which has greatest impact on society while using technology ethically.

    Best AI Hack

    This prize will be given to the hack with the best use of artificial intelligence.

    Best Beginner Hack

    This prize will be given to the best hack created by a group who has never competed in hackathons previously.
